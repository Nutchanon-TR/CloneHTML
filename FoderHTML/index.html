In the Lands Between ruled by Queen Marika the Eternal,
the Elden Ring, the source of the Erdtree, has been shattered.

Marika's offspring, demigods all, claimed the shards of the Elden Ring known as the Great Runes,
and the mad taint of their newfound strength triggered a war: The Shattering.

A war that meant abandonment by the Greater Will.

And now the guidance of grace will be brought to the Tarnished
who were spurned by the grace of gold and exiled from the Lands Between.

Ye dead who yet live, your grace long lost,
follow the path to the Lands Between beyond the foggy sea
to stand before the Elden Ring.

And become the Elden Lord.

A Colossal Realm Teeming with Adventure

Enter an open world featuring endless breathtaking sights and situations,
where the corridors of complex, detailed dungeons stretch in all directions.
What's more—all areas of this vast living, breathing land are seamlessly connected.
In the course of your travels, you will come to know many things: the bliss of discovery,
fearsome towering foes, and the thrill of a hard-earned victory.

A Colossal Realm Teeming with Adventure

Enter an open world featuring endless breathtaking sights and situations,
where the corridors of complex, detailed dungeons stretch in all directions.
What's more—all areas of this vast living, breathing land are seamlessly connected.
In the course of your travels, you will come to know many things: the bliss of discovery,
fearsome towering foes, and the thrill of a hard-earned victory.

Go Forth and Explore—Anywhere and Everywhere

Your Spectral Steed is nimble,
being able to dart from even the steepest of heights at blistering speeds.
Its jumping ability also adds further nuance to the game's profound strategic potential.
Bound through this sprawling world and all its labyrinthine dungeons as you please.
If you can see it, you can explore it.

Sculpt Your Very Own Character

In addition to customizing your player character's appearance,
you can also choose your own unique weapon, gear, and magic loadout.
Will you walk the path of the warrior, cutting down foes with bestial strength?
Or will you seek the path of sorcery and master arcane spells?
Choose how your character grows in accordance with your desired combat style.

Fight With Strategy

In addition to close-quarter blade encounters,
long-range measures such as magic and arrows will also be at your disposal.
Fell your adversaries from the shadows with stealth or summon familiar spirits to even the odds.
Choose from an endless array of strategic options to surmount the trials ahead.

An Epic Story, Woven With the Fabric of Lore and Legends

Communicated in fragments, the game features a deep, meticulously crafted story.
Watch in awe as a drama like no other unfolds from the cast and the conflicts intertwining them.
